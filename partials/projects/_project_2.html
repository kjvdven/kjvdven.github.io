Batavia Stad Newsletter

A system to send out customizable and personal newsetters to subscribed users, maintained from the CMS.

The websites CMS was used to build a newsletter from current offers, news postings and upcomming events. After a newsletter was build, Mailchimp was used to make the newsletter personal and include a BMmember savings balance.

The system consisted of:

  • A CMS section to build modular newsletters;
  • A hook into the Batavia Stad BMember program;
  • Building blocks for a newsletter; and
  • Mailchimp for maintaining subscriptions.
